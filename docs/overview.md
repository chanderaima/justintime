# Just In Time Services

Since 2009, Just In Time Services has provided reliable surface cargo and last-mile delivery solutions across Kochi and surrounding regions. We combine a well-maintained, containerized fleet with experienced drivers and a commitment to statutory compliance to deliver on-time, secure transport for businesses of all sizes.

Our core strengths:

- Operational reliability and safety
- Full regulatory compliance (GST, ESI, Income Tax, Labour Welfare)
- Partnerships with major logistics providers
